# Conjugate Gradient

* **Problem**: $\mathbf{A}\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}$, $\mathbf{A}$ is SPD(symmetric positive-definite)
* **Definition**: $\mathbf{u}$ and $\mathbf{v}$ are **conjugate** if 
$$
\mathbf{u}^T\mathbf{A}\mathbf{v}=0
$$
